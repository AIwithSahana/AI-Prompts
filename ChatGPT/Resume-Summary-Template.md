# PROMPT
---------------
Act as a senior recruiter, ATS optimization expert, and executive resume writer with 15+ years of hiring experience across global companies.

Your task is to create 3 high-impact resume summary versions that are optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and designed to capture recruiter attention within seconds.

CANDIDATE DETAILS
Name:
Target Job Title:
Years of Experience:
Industry:
Key Skills (technical + soft skills):
Top Tools / Technologies:
Key Achievements (with numbers if possible):
Education / Certifications:
Type of Roles Targeting:
Location / Open to Remote?:

JOB DESCRIPTION (Optional but recommended)
Paste the job description here so you can extract important keywords and align the summary with recruiter expectations.

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Analyze the candidate information and the job description.
2. Identify high-value ATS keywords recruiters search for in this role.
3. Naturally integrate those keywords into the summaries.
4. Focus on measurable achievements, business impact, and core strengths.
5. Avoid generic statements like "hardworking" or "team player."
6. Make the summaries powerful, modern, and recruiter-friendly.
7. Ensure clarity, strong positioning, and industry relevance.

OUTPUT FORMAT
First, list the Top ATS Keywords used.
Then generate the following 3 Resume Versions:

1) SIMPLE & CLEAN
- 3–4 lines
- Clear, direct, ATS-friendly
- Strong professional positioning

2) IMPACT-DRIVEN
- 4–5 lines
- Emphasize achievements, metrics, and value delivered
- Strong action-oriented language

3) LEADERSHIP / EXECUTIVE TONE
- 4–5 lines
- Strategic, confident, high-level positioning
- Focus on ownership, results, and influence

WRITING STYLE
- Concise but powerful
- Modern resume language
- No fluff
- ATS readable (no special symbols, no complex formatting)

GOAL
The summaries should make recruiters immediately want to read the rest of the resume.

---------------
