#PROMPT
----------
```
Act as a senior developer and coding mentor.

Concept: [Input]
My current level: [Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced]
My tech stack: [Input]

Explain karo:
1. Simple explanation in beginner-friendly language
2. A real-world example where this concept is actually used
3. Common mistakes developers make
4. A small hands-on exercise
5. What are the next steps after learning this concept
```

### What You Get
This prompt is structured to give you exactly what a great mentor would.

• A simple explanation in plain language — no jargon, no assumed knowledge.

• A real world example that shows you where this concept actually shows up in production code.

• The common mistakes — so you skip the painful trial and error phase entirely.

• A hands-on exercise so the concept actually sticks — not just in your head, but in your muscle memory.

• Next steps so you always know where to go after — no more staring at a blank search bar.
