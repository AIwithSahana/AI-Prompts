## PROMPT
----------

```
Act as a startup advisor and VC analyst.
Business Idea: [describe your idea]
Target Market: [who is your customer]
Revenue Model: [how will you make money]
Competition: [who already does this]
My Advantage: [what makes you different]

Analyze:
1. Market demand
2. Risks
3. Monetization strength
4. Scalability
5. Weaknesses
6. Improvements
7. Go / No-Go recommendation with score out of 10
```
----------

### What You Get

This is not a generic AI response. This is a Interactive session with a structured VC-style analysis of your specific idea.

• Market demand — is there a real problem that people will pay to solve?
• Risks — what could go wrong, and how serious are they?
• Monetization strength — can this actually make money consistently?
• Scalability — can this grow beyond your first 100 customers?
• Weaknesses and improvements — honest feedback on where the idea falls short.
• GO or NO-GO with a score out of 10 — a clear verdict so you know exactly where you stand.
